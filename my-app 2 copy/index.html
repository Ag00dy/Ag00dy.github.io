Splatoon is a motion game that uses gyro controls. Move the controller to aim, and the buttons to shoot(zr), jump,(b) and throw(r). To move, you must use the thumbsticks(L stick).
After exiting the tutorial, you'll be introduced to the games hub world where you can find gear, weapons, food, and the lobby.

The Squidbeak is a hero organization centered around defeating evil threats to the Cephalopod society. Founded by Captain Cuttlefish, this organization operates in all 3 games in Octo Canyon.
Splatoon 2's dlc takes place in the Deepsea Metro where Captain Cuttlefish encounters an Octoling creation chain where he meets the new Agent 8. They come up with a plan to escape the metro along with the help of Off The Hook.
